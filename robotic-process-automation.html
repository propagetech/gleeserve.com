ROBOTIC PROCESS AUTOMATION

Design and Implementation

Proprietary RPA framework for automating workflows of business processes to achieve flexibility & scale with enterprise security.

Scalability

Our orchestrator can be deployed “On-Prem” or “Public Cloud” or “Private Cloud”. Whichever model chosen; we ensure scalability. Our orchestrator can handle synchronous or asynchronous processing of workflows steps with sub-millisecond response times.

Flexibility

Build your workflow task bots in your own language, framework platform as long as it can listen into a configured service bus queue. This model allows you to ring fence your business processes within your infrastructure allowing you to ringfence your proprietary information. Design processes that are auditable, can auto recover and customized for your business. Extract reports with metadata for RPA runs. Analyze data for improving robot performance.

Security

Deployed on Cloud to work within your VPN. Connections to the queue by the Orchestrator or Bots will be over SSL. Workflow meta data is stored in high performance “NoSQL” storage on the cloud which is tuned for high performance reads/writes.
